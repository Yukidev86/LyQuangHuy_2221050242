HTML Entities

Reserved characters must be replaced with HTML entities:

< less than — written as &lt;

> greater than — written as &gt;

& ampersand — written as &amp;

" double quote — written as &quot;

' single quote — written as &apos;

Non-breaking space:

Hello  World!

Other symbols:

© — &copy;

® — &reg;

€ — &euro;

£ — &pound;
